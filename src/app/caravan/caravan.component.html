Caravan ParkWelcome to our Certificated Location (CL) site

Description

Only members of the Caravan and Motorhome Club are allowed to stay on site. There is a five van limit; this includes caravans, motor homes and trailer tents.

There is a shower and flushing toilet on site as well as washing machine and dryer. Shower and laundry operate on a coin box system

Our farm shop can supply campers with fresh lamb, beef, chicken and pork and also our own brand of tea personally selected from our time in Sri Lanka. Fresh home made bread may be available on baking days.

Prices for 2022

£17 per night to include electric hook up, water and an updated environmental charge. This charge is for emptying our waste disposal bin which is provided for campers for general rubbish. We do request that guests separate plastic, glass, papers and tins. Our nightly charge is inclusive for one family of four with car and van or motor home and car.


  • £1 extra for an extra car.
  • £1 extra for a large awning.

We would request that long stay guests use breathable groundsheets and roll them up when out for the day if possible to keep the pitch in good hart for the next camper.
